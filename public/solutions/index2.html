02. Macro Analysis (2025)
Vietnam's FDI Landscape

FDI Inflow Trend (2021-2025)

Source: Ministry of Planning & Investment Source: World Bank Data Source: GSO Vietnam

Analyst Insight

FDI disbursement reached a record high in 2024 (~$23.2B). The shift from pure assembly to High-Tech Processing is accelerating due to Global Minimum Tax regulations.

Key Stat: Manufacturing accounts for >60% of total registered FDI capital.

Drivers

  • China+1 Strategy: Diversifying supply chains.
  • 16 FTAs: Including EVFTA, CPTPP (0% tariffs to EU/US).
  • Labor Cost: Competitive (~$300/mo) vs China (~$1000/mo).

05. Food Industry Specifics
Addressing Unique Challenges

Pain Points

Traceability & Compliance

Strict requirements from FDA, ISO 22000, HACCP for exports.

Cold Chain Breaks

High spoilage (20-25%) in Vietnam due to fragmented logistics.

Vendor Verification

Difficulty validating local supplier capacity and quality.

Our AI Advantage
